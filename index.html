Installing ChessTrack

A quick four-step guide to sideloading on Android.

  1. 1
    Download the APK Tap Start download below. Your phone saves ChessTrack.apk to its Downloads folder.
  2. 2
    Open the file Tap the download notification, or open Files → Downloads and tap ChessTrack.apk.
  3. 3
    Allow install from this source If Android asks, tap Settings, enable Allow from this source, then press back.
  4. 4
    Tap Install Confirm the prompt. In a few seconds ChessTrack is on your home screen, ready to open.
Heads up: Android may warn the file is from an unknown source. That's normal for apps outside the Play Store. ChessTrack collects no data and needs no account.
